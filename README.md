WBA Boxing Knockout Research
Overview
Undergraduate psychological research conducted at The University of Akron analyzing the biological mechanisms behind Loss of Consciousness events in professional boxing.

Research Question
What punch types and impact locations are most associated with knockouts in professional boxing?

Data
Sample of 50 World Boxing Association contests resulting in KO/TKO
Variables recorded: punch type, hand dominance, impact location, round number
Files
Boxing (new).csv — raw dataset
WBABoxing.R — R script used for data analysis and visualization
Visualizations
5 graphs created in R/RStudio demonstrating results of study

Tools Used
R / RStudio
ggplot2
